What It Is

The Newest Ranking Battle in Local Search

AI Recommendation Architecture is the practice of structuring your online presence—schema markup, review data, service pages, and citations—so large language models can confidently understand and recommend your business.

AI tools pull from structured data, review sentiment, and consistent business information across the web. Most home service businesses have none of this in place. We build it, so when a homeowner asks an AI assistant “who's the best roofer near me,” your name comes up first.
